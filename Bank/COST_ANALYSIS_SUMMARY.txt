KEY INSIGHTS:

1. Cost per TPS decreases as you scale (from $5.3K at Stage 1 to $387 at Stage 5)

2. Personnel costs dominate (60-65% of total in later stages)

3. Hidden costs can be 30-50% of visible costs

4. ROI is typically 6-24 months depending on stage and business model

5. Most companies skip Stage 2 (vertical scaling) and go directly from
   Stage 1 → Stage 3 when they hit 80 TPS

6. Biggest cost jump is Stage 3→4 ($1.2M increase) due to geographic
   distribution and compliance requirements

7. Cloud-based deployments are cheaper than on-premise starting at Stage 4

8. Team size grows 3-5x from Stage 1 to Stage 5 (1 person → 15-30 people)
