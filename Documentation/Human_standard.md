# A Simple Human Excellence Framework

A well-rounded person can be evaluated across six dimensions:

1. Health – Physical and mental well-being.
2. Knowledge – Ability to learn and think critically.
3. Character – Integrity, responsibility, and ethics.
4. Relationships – Communication and cooperation.
5. Contribution – Creating value for family, work, and society.
6. Growth – Continuous improvement and lifelong learning.
