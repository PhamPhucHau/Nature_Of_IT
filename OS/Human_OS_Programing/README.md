## Key Concepts

### 1. Task Management
Tasks are like processes in an OS. They have:
- **ID**: Unique identifier
- **Priority**: Importance level
- **Duration**: Estimated time
- **Deadline**: When it must be done
- **Status**: Current state (pending, running, completed)
- **Energy**: Required energy level

### 2. Energy Management
Energy is like CPU resources. It has:
- **Mental Energy**: For thinking and decision-making
- **Physical Energy**: For movement and action
- **Emotional Energy**: For relationships and motivation
- **Recovery**: Time to restore energy

### 3. Decision Making
Decisions are made by:
- **Analyzing** current state
- **Evaluating** options
- **Considering** constraints
- **Choosing** best option
- **Executing** decision

### 4. Learning
Learning happens through:
- **Storing** experiences
- **Analyzing** patterns
- **Extracting** lessons
- **Updating** knowledge
- **Improving** strategies
